Garden 360

About

A concept project for a vegetable garden in the heart of the city, designed to feed the community while bringing green space to an urban environment and repurposing surplus parking spaces.

Purpose

Each plant in the garden is accompanied by a custom QR code label that links to this website with more information about the plant and the garden itself.
